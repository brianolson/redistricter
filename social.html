why the odd links?
Facebook and Google+ have the ability to track you around the internet when you visit any page that has a [like] or [+1] button on it. I don't like that. So, at this site, those buttons only appear when you ask for them (when you're about to actually use them). Also, it makes the page load slightly faster. (Twitter and Reddit use more traditional links that only act when clicked on.)
